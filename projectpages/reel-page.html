OBJECTIVE AND INSPIRATION

WHAT:

A one minute video highlighting my motion design work from throughout the full semester of the course

WHY: To practice pacing, storytelling, and thoughtful editing, culminating in the creation of a self-marketing piece to use in the future

PROCESS | STORYBOARD

After reviewing all of my motion design projects, I chose the pieces I felt were strongest. From there, I considered how they could compliment one another and work together through elements of shape, color, and movement. Part of this process involved storyboarding. The storyboards shown above were helpful in mapping out the frames and imagining possible cuts. However, I did end up deviating from the order in the storyboards here and there based on timing and ideas that came to me once I began working with the music.

OUTCOME | VIDEO

The final product, shown above, features pieces that involve 3D animation, infographics, assemblage-sytle animation, kinetic typography, kinetic abstraction, and television bumper design.
